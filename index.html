Hello,

I'm Kondapally Madhavi, a PhD scholar in Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Hyderabad, under the guidance of Dr. C. Krishna Mohan. My research centers on Autonomous Vehicle Technology.

I completed my Master's degree in Computer Science and Engineering at JNTU Hyderabad and B.Tech at Kakatiya University, Warangal. I am a passionate researcher in the fields of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML), with a strong emphasis on computer vision. I actively contribute to open-source projects and enjoy collaborating with the research community.

I have made notable contributions to my field through publications in respected venues such as the IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems, the International Joint Conference on Neural Networks (IJCNN) and the International Conference on Pattern Recognition (ICPR) Workshops. I have also developed a transitional weather dataset for autonomous driving, filed two patents, and served as an external reviewer for renowned platforms like IJCNN and journals including Neurocomputing.
